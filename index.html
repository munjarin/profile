About Me

Hello! I am Munjarin Rahman

Location : San Francisco, California, United States

I am an entrepreneur and a master’s student in business analytics at Hult International Business School. With 11 years of experience in global supply chain management within the textile industry, I founded and served as the Group Managing Director of Tex Merchant PTE Limited (Singapore), Tex Merchant Bangladesh, and TxM Lifestyle Limited. My academic journey began with a degree in Textile Technology in 2012, followed by post-graduation studies at Taylor’s Business School and the Chartered Management Institute. As a Chartered Management Institute member, I possess a deep understanding of business strategy and management principles. Before launching Tex Merchant, I worked as a product developer for renowned brands. Additionally, I’ve contributed to both academic and non-academic publications as a researcher and author. I'm also a musician. Currently, I hold shares and directorships in several start-up companies, including The Cartels Limited (a cloud kitchen in Bangladesh) and Infiniti Sphere IT (a Research Journal Management System in Singapore), among others. My passion lies in sustainable business practices and advocating for green energy. Beyond business, I’m dedicated to children’s education and animal rights. I’m open to connecting for exchange opportunities, learning, and networking.
